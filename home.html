Experiences

Here are my experiences with the coding bootcamp so far:

  • The pace is good - I find the discussion easy to follow.
  • There are many examples provided.
  • Questions are answered and assistance is immediately provided to anyone from the class.
  • Explanations are clear; plenty of information is provided about each element and item being discussed.

If I were to rank the best features of the coding bootcamp, it would be in the following sequence:

  1. Accessibility - free and uses apps/tools that are easy to access
  2. Clarity - very easy to understand for beginners like me
  3. Pace - discussions are easy to follow
